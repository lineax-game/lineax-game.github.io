About the Project

LINEAX was born from a desire to create a puzzle experience that strips away the noise. The vision was simple: a minimalist logic game where clean geometry meets a vibrant, strategic use of color.

At its core, LINEAX introduces a specific spatial mechanism—a challenge of chromatic balance where every triangular cell demands a unique harmony of tones. It is designed for those who find beauty in order and satisfaction in the quiet click of a solved mind-bender.

The Philosophy

In a world full of mindless distractions, LINEAX stands as a meaningful alternative. It is designed not just to entertain, but to sharpen logical thinking and spatial awareness. Instead of shallow mechanics that offer no lasting benefit, this game provides a genuine cognitive workout. To ensure a pure, undisturbed experience, LINEAX is completely free of advertisements—allowing you to focus entirely on the flow of the puzzle and the growth of your mind.
